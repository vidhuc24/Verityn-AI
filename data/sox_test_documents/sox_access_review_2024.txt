Period: Q4 2024

================================================================================

Executive Summary:
This access review evaluates user access controls across critical financial systems to ensure compliance with SOX 404 requirements for the quarter ending December 31, 2024.

Scope:
Review covers ERP system, financial reporting platform, and treasury management system access for Q4 2024.

Key Findings:
• 3 users identified with excessive privileges requiring immediate remediation
• 2 orphaned accounts found and disabled
• Access review process needs enhancement for quarterly reviews
• Segregation of duties matrix updated for 2025

Risk Assessment:
Medium risk level due to excessive privileges and process gaps

Recommendations:
• Implement role-based access controls (RBAC) by Q1 2025
• Establish quarterly access review process
• Enhance monitoring for privileged user activities
• Update access request and approval workflows

Compliance Status:
Partially compliant - requires remediation by Q1 2025
